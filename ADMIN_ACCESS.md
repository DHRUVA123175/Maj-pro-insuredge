   - Use admin credentials above

   - Track their claim history

3. **Manage Claims:**
   - Click "All Claims" tab
   - View detailed claim information
   - Click "View" to see full details with AI analysis

4. **Monitor Activity:**
   - Click "Recent Activity" tab
   - See latest claims in real-time
